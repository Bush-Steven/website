About Us

Transforming Africa
Through Technology

Founded in 2015, AfriQueue Technologies has grown from a small Nairobi startup to one of East Africa's most trusted technology partners.

We are a team of passionate technologists, engineers, and innovators dedicated to solving Africa's most pressing operational challenges. Our journey began with a simple mission: to eliminate long queues and waiting times in public and private institutions across the continent.

Today, we serve over 50 clients across Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda — delivering smart queue management systems, telemedicine platforms, cybersecurity training, CCTV installations, and comprehensive IT infrastructure solutions.

Our Mission

To empower African organizations with intelligent, affordable technology solutions that improve efficiency, security, and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading technology solutions provider, driving digital transformation one organization at a time.
